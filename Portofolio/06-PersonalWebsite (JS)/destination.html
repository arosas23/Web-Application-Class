My Favorite Spots



Guadalajara

Guadalajara is the cultural heart of Mexico, famous for mariachi music, tequila, and beautiful colonial architecture. The city blends tradition with modern life and vibrant festivals.

Fun Facts:

Jalisco

Mexico

~1.5 million (metro)

20.6597° N, 103.3496° W

Birthplace of mariachi and tequila.

Tokyo

Tokyo is a dynamic city where ancient shrines coexist with futuristic skyscrapers. It’s known for its cuisine, cutting-edge technology, and bustling urban life.

Fun Facts:

Tokyo Metropolis

Japan

~37 million (metro)

35.6762° N, 139.6503° E

Home to the busiest train station in the world (Shinjuku).

London

London is a historic yet modern city, home to landmarks like Big Ben, the London Eye, and Buckingham Palace. It offers a unique mix of culture, history, and global influence.

Fun Facts:

Greater London

United Kingdom

~9 million (metro)

51.5074° N, 0.1278° W

Home to the British Museum, which houses a vast collection of world art and artifacts. London has more than 170 museums.

Barcelona

Barcelona is a Mediterranean city famous for Gaudí’s architecture, lively beaches, and a rich artistic spirit.

Fun Facts:

Catalonia

New York City

New York City, “the city that never sleeps,” is known for Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty.

Fun Facts:

New York
