Background

I grew up in a small family with my father and mother. Since I was little, I have loved drawing and even painting on the walls of our house. When I was six years old, my father took me to an art class, which I truly enjoyed. I continued taking art classes until I was fifteen years old. So, when it came time to choose my career, I was certain it would be something related to creativity.

My main goal as a professional is to keep learning new and innovative things in the field of animation. I would love to work in the industry, collaborating on movies and bringing stories to life through art and technology.
