My Favorite Spots

Guadalajara

Guadalajara is the cultural heart of Mexico, famous for mariachi music, tequila, and beautiful colonial architecture. The city blends tradition with modern life and vibrant festivals.

Fun Facts:

  • Providence/State: Jalisco
  • Country: México
  • Population: ~1.5 million (metro)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 20.6597° N, 103.3496° W
  • Fact: Guadalajara is considered the birthplace of mariachi music and tequila.

Tokyo

Tokyo is a dynamic city where ancient shrines coexist with futuristic skyscrapers. It’s known for its cuisine, cutting-edge technology, and bustling urban life.

Fun Facts:

  • Providence/State: Tokyo Metropolis
  • Country: Japan
  • Population: ~37 million (metro)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 35.6762° N, 139.6503° E
  • Fact: Tokyo is home to the busiest train station in the world, Shinjuku Station, with over 3.5 million passengers daily.

London

London is a historic yet modern city, home to landmarks like Big Ben, the London Eye, and Buckingham Palace. It offers a unique mix of culture, history, and global influence.

Fun Facts:

  • Providence/State: Greater London
  • Country: United Kingdom
  • Population: ~9 million (city)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 51.5072° N, 0.1276° W
  • Fact: London has over 170 museums, including the world-famous British Museum.

Barcelona

Barcelona is a Mediterranean city famous for Gaudí’s architecture, lively beaches, and a rich artistic spirit. It’s a perfect blend of history, creativity, and nightlife.

Fun Facts:

  • Providence/State: Catalonia
  • Country: Spain
  • Population: ~1.6 million (city), ~5.6 million (metro)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 41.3874° N, 2.1686° E
  • Fact: Barcelona’s Sagrada Família has been under construction for more than 140 years and is still not finished.

New York City

New York City, “the city that never sleeps,” is known for Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty. It’s a global hub of culture, finance, and entertainment.

Fun Facts:

  • Providence/State: New York
  • Country: United States
  • Population: ~8.5 million (city), ~19 million (metro)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 40.7128° N, 74.0060° W
  • Fact: New York City is made up of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island.
